I help property management companies and landlords cut admin work, fill vacancies faster, and run cleaner operations by pairing hands-on PM expertise with AI workflow automation. I do not just coordinate the work. I build the tools, websites, and workflows that make it run on autopilot.

### What I do

- **Property operations:** leasing and tenant coordination, maintenance and work-order ops, vendor dispatch, rent and delinquency tracking, owner reporting.
- **AI workflow automation:** lead-nurture sequences, Claude assistants, listing copy and lease summaries, SOPs wired across your PMS, email, and spreadsheets.
- **Build:** property management websites, leasing funnels, landing pages, and internal tools.
